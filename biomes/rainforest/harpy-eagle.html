Harpy Eagle

Sky king of the canopy

← Back to Rainforest Animals
Rainforest

Strong wings and sharper claws

Harpy eagles soar through the highest branches searching for monkeys and sloths. Their grip is stronger than a bear's paw.

Mighty Talons

Huge curved claws can grab and hold heavy prey, even animals as big as sloths.

Broad Wings

Short, wide wings let the eagle zip between trees without bumping branches.

Feather Crown

Feathers around its face form a dish that helps the eagle hear tiny sounds of hiding animals.

Harpy eagles are gentle giants to their chicks but fierce hunters in flight.

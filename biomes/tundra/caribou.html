Tundra

Marching with the herd

Caribou migrate thousands of miles each year to find fresh plants and soft moss to eat.

Hoof Snowshoes

Wide hooves spread out like snowshoes and have sharp edges for digging through ice to reach food.

Seasonal Coats

Thick winter fur keeps them warm, while shorter summer fur helps them stay cool.

Migrating Instinct

Caribou remember safe routes and follow older leaders to food and calving grounds.

Caribou teach us about long-distance teamwork.

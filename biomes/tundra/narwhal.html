Mystery in the waves

Narwhals swim in Arctic waters in small pods. Their long tusks are actually special teeth.

Sensitive Tusks

The tusk is covered in tiny holes that help narwhals sense water temperature and pressure.

Deep Divers

Narwhals can dive a mile deep to find fish and squid.

Blubber Power

Thick blubber keeps them warm during long dives in freezing water.

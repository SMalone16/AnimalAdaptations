Working as a team

Arctic wolves travel in packs to hunt musk oxen and hares. They roam far across the tundra.

Shared Hunts

Packs cooperate to track and surround prey, sharing the food afterward.

Warm Coats

Thick fur and small ears keep heat inside their bodies.

Long Journeys

Wolves can travel many miles each day to follow migrating herds.

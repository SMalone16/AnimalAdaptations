Tundra

Staying warm in snow

Arctic foxes trot across icy tundra searching for lemmings, eggs, and leftovers left by polar bears.

Winter Coat

Thick white fur traps warm air close to the fox's body and blends with snow.

Furry Feet

Fur on the paws works like snow boots, keeping toes warm and giving grip on ice.

Color Change

In summer the fur turns brown so the fox can hide among rocks and plants.

Cozy fur keeps the arctic fox comfy in cold weather.

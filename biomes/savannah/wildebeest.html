Savannah

Following the rains

Wildebeest travel in one of the largest animal migrations on Earth. They move with the rains to find fresh grass.

Endurance Bodies

Strong hearts and muscles let them run for miles without getting tired.

Group Protection

Huge herds make it hard for predators to catch any one wildebeest.

Calf Synchrony

Most babies are born at the same time so there are many eyes to protect them.

Traveling together keeps wildebeest safe and fed.

Sharing the savannah

Lions are the only big cats that live in families called prides. They rest together and work together to hunt large prey.

Cooperative Hunting

Lionesses circle prey from different sides, using teamwork to bring down animals bigger than themselves.

Night Vision

Special eyes gather light, helping lions see clearly in the dark when it is coolest.

Roaring Voices

Deep roars travel miles across the plains to warn strangers and keep the pride together.

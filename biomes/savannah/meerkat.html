Savannah

Busy burrow life

Meerkats live in big families called mobs. They dig tunnels to stay safe from the hot sun and hungry predators.

Lookout Duty

One meerkat stands tall to watch for danger while the others dig and forage.

Digging Claws

Sharp claws scoop out burrows quickly so the family can hide from the heat.

Dust Goggles

Clear patches around their eyes block the sun and keep sand out while they dig.

Sharing chores keeps meerkat mobs happy.

Desert

Leaping for life

Kangaroo rats hop like tiny kangaroos. They gather seeds and store them in cool burrows.

Water From Food

Their bodies turn seeds into water, so they almost never need to drink.

Super Jumps

Strong back legs let them leap away from snakes in a flash.

Seed Pantries

They hide seeds in underground rooms, saving snacks for dry spells.

Hopping and storing keep kangaroo rats healthy.

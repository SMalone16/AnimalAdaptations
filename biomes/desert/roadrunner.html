Zipping over hot ground

Roadrunners dash between cactus plants chasing insects, lizards, and even rattlesnakes.

Speedy Legs

Strong leg muscles let roadrunners sprint up to 20 miles per hour.

Cooling Tricks

They open their beaks and flutter their throat skin to release heat, like a built-in fan.

Cactus Perches

Roadrunners hop onto cactus tops to spot prey and look out for danger.

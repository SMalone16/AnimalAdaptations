Desert

Cool in the heat

Fennec foxes stay active at night in the Sahara Desert. Their enormous ears and thick fur keep them comfortable.

Ear Radiators

Blood flows through their huge ears to cool down the fox's body like a built-in fan.

Nighttime Schedule

Fennec foxes sleep in burrows during the hot day and hunt insects and fruit at night when it is cooler.

Furry Feet

Thick fur on their paws protects them from hot sand and gives grip on dunes.
